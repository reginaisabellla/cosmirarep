OUR VISION

Cosmira is dedicated to providing a flawless base makeup solution that matches every skin tone perfectly. Our company focuses on finding diverse skin types and shades, ensuring that every individual can achieve a seamless and natural-looking finish. With a commitment to quality and inclusivity, Cosmira aims to revolutionize the beauty industry by offering a range of base makeup options that enhance the beauty of all skin tones.

MISSION

We at Cosmira are dedicated to ensuring anyone who wishes to utilize makeup can do so without the frustration of being excluded due to factors out of our member's control.
Cosmira strives to maintain a positive environment that encourages users from any level of experience to get involved with the community and help push makeup companies to focus more on creating quality products rather than quick cash grabs.

VALUES

Cosmira places a great emphasis on maintaining an inclusive environment for anyone who wants to participate.
We have a ZERO tolerance policy for hate speech and threats of violence.
Cosmira is dedicated to its members right to self-expression and encourages users to present themselves authentically
